ACID BRAIN

ACID BRAIN

A heavy psychedelic band based in Taipei, formed in 2023 by four high school students...

Blending elements of Stoner Rock, Sludge Metal, Doom Metal, Psychedelic Rock, Post-Rock, Space Rock, and Progressive Rock...

Acid Brain doesn’t just perform music—they invite listeners into their world, to experience an unforgettable journey.

SELECTED PERFORMANCES

2023

  • 06/07 — NOISE in My Head, Revolver, Taipei
    Acid Brain’s debut show, performed at one of Taipei’s most iconic underground venues.
  • 08/05 — Explosivedb Fest, Mingtai Paper Factory, Taipei
    A DIY festival co-curated by an Acid Brain member, featuring Taiwan’s loud scene—psychedelic, noise, post-rock, stoner rock, and more.
  • 10/27–29 — Unlimited Freedom Festival ’23, Yen’s Ranch, Nantou
    One of Taiwan’s long-running outdoor festivals. Acid Brain’s first show outside of Taipei.
  • 12/03 — Taipei Psychedelic Rock Festival Vol.2: Aftershow, Continue Music Studio
    A highly praised festival dedicated to psychedelic music. Following the success of its first edition, it became a local favorite in Taipei’s psych scene.

2024

  • 03/20 — Li Zenghui Live in Taipei “Sea Eat!”, The UU Mouth
    Acid Brain was invited to perform at the Taipei stop of the Chinese famous experimental saxophonist and poet Li Zenghui’s solo tour, blending electronics, improvisation, and poetry.
  • 04/19 — Green Milk From The Planet Orange Taiwan Tour, Revolver, Taipei
    Acid Brain joined the Taiwan tour of legendary Japanese psychedelic/prog rock band Green Milk From The Planet Orange as a supporting act.
  • 05/09 — Virgin Vacation “Dapple Patterns” Asia Tour, The Wall Live House, Taipei
    The Taipei show of Hong Kong’s experimental band Virgin Vacation, known for blending electronics, space rock, and post-punk. Acid Brain joined as a supporting act.
  • 07/04 — The Next Big Thing (Vol. 5), Legacy Taipei
    A major Taipei showcase for emerging artists. One of Acid Brain’s most defining performances on a large professional stage.
  • 08/17 — Collapse into Lethe, Continue Music Studio, Taipei
    Organized by underground punk collective Suck Glue Boys. Acid Brain performed alongside Southeast Asian doom bands and local experimental noise artists.
  • 08/24 — Park Park Carnival, Taipei Expo Park
    One of Taipei’s prominent outdoor festivals, featuring a wide range of independent artists across genres.
  • 10/04 — Vagabond Fest – Eve Show, TCRC Live House, Tainan
    The pre-festival event of Vagabond Fest, one of southern Taiwan’s most iconic music events. Acid Brain performed alongside both local and international acts.
  • 12/28 — Breakingmudfest, Xinyue Plaza, Taipei
    A long-running outdoor punk-rooted music festival held in both Taipei and Kaohsiung, supported by a strong local DIY community.

2025

  • 02/15 — 野地晃晃, Fat Tone Music / Wild Lab, Taipei
    An event held across Fat Tone Music (a local rehearsal studio) and Wild Lab—two raw and familiar underground spaces in Taipei.
  • 02/27 — Conan Asia Tour, Jack Music, Taipei
    Acid Brain joined the Taipei stop of UK doom metal legends Conan’s 2025 Asia Tour. One of the band’s most defining performances to date.
  • 06/28 — Bangkog Post 2025, Mr.FOX Livehouse, Bangkok
    Acid Brain’s first overseas show, performing as part of Bangkok’s well-known underground scene.
